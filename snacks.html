CRISPY CORN CHAT

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup crispy corn
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon

PROCEDURE:

  1. In a bowl, add crispy corn, onions, tomatoes, coriander leaves, and green chilies.
  2. Then add chaat masala, salt, and lemon juice.
  3. mix it well
  4. Serve immediately as a snack
